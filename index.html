Lead Generation

Lead generation is the process of gaining the interest of potential customers in order to increase future sales. It is a crucial part of the sales process of many companies. A lead is anyone who has shown interest in a companys products or services but may not yet be qualified to buy. They are potential customers with whom a company has not yet done business, but who have given reason to believe they may want to in the future.
